What You Can Do to Support SDG 6

Sustainable Development Goal 6 focuses on ensuring access to clean water and sanitation for all. Every person, from students to educators, can make a real difference through small, intentional actions. Here’s how:

1. Individual Actions

Each of us can contribute to water sustainability in our daily lives. Some effective actions include:

  • Turning off taps while brushing teeth or washing dishes
  • Fixing leaky faucets and using water-efficient appliances
  • Choosing eco-friendly cleaning and hygiene products
  • Practicing good personal hygiene to prevent waterborne illnesses

2. Community Involvement

Communities have power when people come together for a common cause. Get involved by:

  • Organizing or participating in local clean water awareness campaigns
  • Collaborating with your school to start hygiene education programs
  • Volunteering with NGOs that work on water and sanitation projects

3. Support Water Organizations

Many nonprofits and global organizations are dedicated to improving water access. You can support them by:

  • Donating to charities like Water.org or UNICEF’s WASH program
  • Raising awareness about their work on social media
  • Hosting fundraisers or drives in your school or community
Students, schools, and local communities can create lasting impact by adopting water-saving habits, spreading knowledge, and supporting ongoing efforts to provide clean water and sanitation for all.
