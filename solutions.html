Global Water Crisis and Sanitation

Solutions & Recommendations

  1. Investment in Infrastructure

    Build and maintain water pipelines, purification systems and toilets - especially in rural and low-income areas.

  2. Pollution Control

    Enforce laws to prevent industrial dumping and improve wastewater treatment.

  3. Policy and Global Cooperation
    • Implement integrated water resources management(WRM).
    • Support global goals like UN Sustainable Development Goal 6:Clean Water and Sanitation for All.
