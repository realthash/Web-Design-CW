What You Can Do to Support SDG 6

Ensuring availability and sustainable management of water and sanitation for all

Individual Actions Community Involvement Support Organizations Students, Schools & Communities

Individual Actions

Start making an impact at home by being mindful of how you use water and the products you consume:

  • Turn off taps while brushing your teeth or doing dishes.
  • Fix leaking pipes and faucets to conserve water.
  • Use water-efficient appliances like low-flow showerheads.
  • Choose eco-friendly and biodegradable cleaning products.
  • Maintain proper hygiene to prevent waterborne diseases.

Community Involvement

When communities unite for a cause, the effect multiplies. Get involved with initiatives around you:

  • Organize or attend local water conservation awareness campaigns.
  • Implement school or community water-saving programs.
  • Volunteer in local clean water projects or sanitation drives.

Support Organizations

Many global organizations are committed to improving water and sanitation. Here's how you can help:

  • Donate to NGOs that build wells and toilets in underdeveloped areas.
  • Promote fundraising efforts and charity events supporting SDG 6.
  • Spread the word about water crises around the world.

Students, Schools & Communities

Small steps taken by students and institutions can lead to powerful changes:

  • Start a school club focused on clean water and sanitation awareness.
  • Host exhibitions or contests promoting responsible water use.
  • Partner with NGOs to bring workshops or speakers to school events.
  • Schools can install rainwater harvesting systems and water-saving devices.
